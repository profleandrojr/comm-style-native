## üß† About the Project

This tool digitalizes the communication style assessment to help professionals understand how they negotiate.

### The 3 Styles:

- üî¥ **Red (Hierarchical):** Action-oriented, focused on results and speed.
- üîµ **Blue (Visionary):** People-oriented, focused on relationships and empathy.
- üü¢ **Green (Pragmatic):** Data-oriented, focused on processes and analysis.

### Key Features:

- **Interactive Assessment:** 27 dynamic questions to determine your profile.
- **Anti-Bias Algorithm:** Answer options are randomized every time to prevent pattern guessing.
- **Real-time Analysis:** Instant scoring with animated result charts.
- **Viral Loop:** Native integration to share your specific result directly to X (Twitter).
- **Data Persistence:** Progress is saved automatically; close the app and resume exactly where you left off.
